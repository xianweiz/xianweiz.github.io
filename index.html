> About

Xianwei works in AMD Inc. since 2017 as a compute architect/researcher. He completed his Ph.D in the Computer Science Department at University of Pittsburgh, and obtained his Bachelor's degree on Software Engineering from Northwestern Polytechnical University. He had been research intern and development intern in Nvidia and Alibaba, respectively. More info can be found in LinkedIn.

> Research

Topics: GPU, Memory System, Intelligent Computing, HPC, Simulation/Modeling/Profiling
Xianwei's research interests lie broadly in hardware and software co-designs to improve the performance and energy efficiency of computing systems. A particular emphasis is on memory system and GPU computing design around the critical aspects of latency, energy and bandwidth, etc., for future Exascale supercomputing and Intelligent computing.
